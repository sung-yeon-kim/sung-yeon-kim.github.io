Sungyeon Kim

Ph.D. @ Computer Vison LAB in POSTECH.

  sungyeon.kim@postech.ac.kr

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Computer Vision Lab at POSTECH (Pohang University of Science and Technology). I will join Amazon Visual Search Team as an Applied Research Scientist starting in July 2025. I received Ph.D. at Computer Science and Engineering, POSTECH under the supervision of Prof. Suha Kwak. My research focuses on deep metric learning, representation learning, retrieval, and their applications.
