\section{Summary}
\small{
I am an Applied Scientist II at AWS Agentic AI, where I work on \href{https://docs.aws.amazon.com/bedrock-agentcore/latest/devguide/evaluations.html}{AgentCore Evaluation} and \href{https://github.com/strands-agents}{Strands Agents}. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from \href{https://www.postech.ac.kr/eng/}{POSTECH}, advised by \href{http://cvlab.postech.ac.kr/~suhakwak/}{Prof. Suha Kwak}. At AWS, my work spans agent evaluation, reliability signals, evaluation methodology, and multimodal evaluators. Previously at Amazon Core Search, I contributed to \href{https://www.aboutamazon.com/news/retail/search-image-amazon-lens-live-shopping-rufus}{Amazon Lens Live} through multimodal retrieval, result verification, and MLLM-based re-ranking. My research focuses on reliable agentic AI systems, agent evaluation, agentic retrieval, multimodal retrieval, MLLM-based retrieval/ranking, long-horizon LLM agents, agent memory and context management, and representation learning, with side interests in VLA robot learning and RL (PPO/GRPO).
}
